The Conquest of Alexander the Great

Alexander the Great's conquests extended across most of the known world to the ancient Greeks. The great scale of the battles despite the size of Alexander's army has marked him to be one of the most successful and celebrated military commanders in history. This resource will explore the various campaigns and battles during his 13 years of conquest.
